Hello! I'm Amanda, a dedicated agile facilitator, product owner, and full stack web developer with a passion for empowering others. My journey into the world of web development began with a strong desire to create meaningful digital experiences. Since graduating high school in 2014, I've embarked on various life adventures, including becoming a wife and mom in 2016, which has profoundly shaped my approach to both personal and professional growth.

In 2019, I achieved certification as an Agile Facilitator, Coach, and Product Owner, which allowed me to fully embrace agile methodologies and lead with a servant leadership mindset. This journey continued as I took on the role of Product Director at Balanced Agility in 2020, where I work closely with teams to deliver value-driven solutions.

I graduated from Bellevue University in 2025 with a bachelors degree in web development. This enhanced my technical skills, which I now apply to my work with even greater passion and precision. One of my deepest passions is supporting and growing women-owned and minority-owned businesses, as well as non-profits, particularly within my beloved home state of Nebraska. I am committed to using my web development skills to build platforms that empower these communities and help them thrive.

Whether it's through developing robust websites or offering agile coaching, my goal is to make a positive impact and contribute to a vibrant, inclusive economy. Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me and my journey. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
